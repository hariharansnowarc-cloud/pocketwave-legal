How it works

01

Pick your city

Sign up and choose your city. Your feed instantly fills with posts from locals around you.

02

Post what matters

Share news, ask questions, flag issues, or start a discussion. Anything relevant to your neighbourhood.

03

Vote & discuss

Upvote what's important, downvote the noise. Reply in threaded comments to keep things organised.

04

Build your karma

Earn karma as your posts get upvoted. High-karma locals become trusted voices in the community.

What you get

◆

Hyperlocal feed

Only posts from your city. No national noise, no algorithm nonsense — just your neighbourhood.

◆

Hot / New / Top sorting

Browse trending posts, freshest content, or all-time greats with a single click.

◆

Nested comments

Threaded replies keep discussions readable. Reply to anyone, at any depth.

◆

Real-time updates

Votes and comments update live. No refresh needed — the feed breathes on its own.

◆

Zero ads. Ever.

No advertisers, no sponsored posts, no data harvesting. Just the community.
